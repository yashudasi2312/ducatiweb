Multistrada

REWRITING THE RULES OF TRAVEL

Rewrite the rules of travel

Touring ready, intuitive off-road, agile in the city, and with renewed sports attitude, the fourth generation of Multistrada is pure riding pleasure. Lightweight, compact and with the 170 hp of the V4 Granturismo, it promises a sporty and exhilarating ride on mixed surfaces.

Available in Arctic White, Ducati Red and Pearl Black.

ERGONOMICS

A dedicated riding position has been designed for the Multistrada to ensure a sporty and effective riding position.

Adventure together

The Multistrada V4 Rally is the ideal bike for two-up travel and has been designed to maximise the level of comfort for the rider and their travel companion. The windscreen has been redesigned and the case attachment now faces rearward to offer the passenger more room. The vast range of rider and passenger seats available as accessories ensure real ergonomic flexibility.

Brakes

The Ducati Multistrada features high-performance Brembo brakes, ensuring exceptional stopping power and control in all riding conditions. Equipped with dual 320mm or 330mm front discs paired with Brembo Stylema or M50 calipers, the braking system delivers strong and responsive performance

Engine

The Ducati Multistrada is powered by a high-performance V4 Granturismo engine, designed for both power and durability. This 1,158cc, liquid-cooled, 90-degree V4 engine delivers strong mid-range torque and high-revving power, making it perfect for long-distance touring and spirited riding. Unlike traditional Desmodromic systems, it features a spring valve return system, reducing maintenance and extending service intervals to 60,000 km.

TFT Display

The Ducati Multistrada features a high-resolution TFT display, offering a modern and intuitive interface for riders. Depending on the model, the display ranges from 5-inch to 6.5-inch with full-color graphics and excellent visibility in all lighting conditions. It provides real-time information such as speed, RPM, gear position, fuel level, and riding modes.
