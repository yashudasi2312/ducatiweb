Experience pure adrenaline with the Ducati Monster—a masterpiece of Italian engineering built for those who crave power, style, and agility. With its muscular design, lightweight frame, and high-performance L-twin engine, the Monster delivers an electrifying ride that dominates both city streets and open highways. Precision handling, aggressive styling, and heart-pounding acceleration make every ride unforgettable. Whether you're carving corners or making a statement on the road, the Ducati Monster is more than just a motorcycle—it's a legend reborn.

01. Lightweight

The aluminium Front Frame contributes to keeping the dry weight down to just 166 kg. Lightness is key to ensuring the utmost enjoyment aboard a bike that is easy to handle, fast and, above all, fun.

02. Compact

Narrow sides and a front frame that ends at the height of the vertical cylinder head ensure the rider can easily touch the ground with their feet when stationary. The muscular bison-back tank for which the Monster stands out is re-proportioned, now more compact to ensure more comfortable and effective ergonomics.

03. Essential

A design that is all about simple, clean lines. An engine, a saddle, a tank, and a handlebar. Everything you need to ride and have fun, nothing more.

04. Fun

The Monster mounts the Euro 5 approved 937 cc Testastretta 11° engine with desmodromic timing. Delivering 111 hp @9250 rpm, the engine is smooth and controlled at low speeds, navigating traffic without hesitation, but also dynamic at mid to high speeds, for electrifying performance in every situation. Its contained weight contributes to the bike’s overall lightness and heightens rideability.

Even more sports attitude

In its new colour combination, the pure white and bright red selected for the seat upholstery highlight the simple, clean lines of the Monster. The red tags on the wheels add a sophisticated, sporty touch.
