TIMELY BLACK STYLE

The Icon Dark embraces minimalism with a bold, all-black design. From the matte black painted fuel tank to the black-painted plastics on the mudguard, fuel tank cover, and headlight covers, every element exudes understated elegance.

LIGHTWEIGHT & NIMBLE

Designed for easy handling, the Icon Dark boasts a lightweight chassis and engine, making it agile and responsive in any riding condition. The Nightshift/Full Throttle tail, anodized handlebar clamp, and full black timing belt covers enhance its stripped-down aesthetic, keeping it light yet robust.

MODERN TECH

Equipped with cutting-edge technology, including a 4.3” TFT display, Ride-by-Wire, and modern rider aids, the Icon Dark ensures a smooth, safe, and connected ride. The X-Shaped DRL and full LED lighting system provide excellent visibility, adding a touch of modernity to its classic design.
