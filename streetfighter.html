AERODYNAMICS

Streetfighter V4 is an even sportier and more dynamic, pushing the Fight Formula concepts even further forward. More exciting in aesthetics, more thrilling to ride, and even lighter.

DESIGN

Its aggressive and minimalist aesthetic features a front inspired by the Joker of the comics, the Streetfighter V4 immediately entered the hearts of enthusiasts.

ELECTRONICS

The electronics package is richer and more effective with new engine delivery curves differentiated gear by gear, Wet Riding Mode, and new Track Evo display for the TFT dashboard.

CHASSIS AND ERGONOMICS

The chassis layout includes a Front Frame and single-sided aluminum swingarm. The new shape of the tank gives more support to the rider when braking and running.

ENGINE

The 1,103 cc Desmosedici Stradale has been revised in calibration to match the larger diameter of the silencer outlet, introduced in order to reduce the exhaust back pressure.

ACCESSORIES

The wide range of accessories allows you to customize the Streetfighter V4, making it even sportier, even for those who want to use it on the track.

Customize your Streetfighter

Customize your Streetfighter V4 by making it even sportier: exhaust silencers, mudguards, fins and frame and crankcase protectors in carbon fiber, as well as thermal blankets and stands for those who want to use their Streetfighter V4 on the track.
